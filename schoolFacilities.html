School Facilities

We know school facilities affect health, behavior, engagement, efforts, and achievement of the students, so our training grounds and classrooms are designed to facilitate learning of all Kung Fu styles. The school put alot of efforts to provide a training environment that is comfortable, secure, safe, accessible and pleasing for both our students and teachers. The school has a wide range of excellent facilities including:
  • Sand Bag Training Groun
  • Wooden Pile Training Ground
  • Large Endurance Training Ground
  • Palm Blossom Pile Training Ground
  • Suits And Uniforms
  • Bags And Holders
  • Wushu Footwear
  • Wing Chun Dummies
  • Glovers And Wraps
  • Sashes
  • All Kung Fu Weaponss
  • Double Target Pads
  • Punching Bags
Other Facilities include:
  • Classroom For Learning Chinese Instruments
  • Classroom For Learning Chinese Calligraphy And Linyi City Is Known As The City Of Calligraphy
  • Free Wireless Connection
  • 3 Meals Per Day
  • Laundry And Cloth Drying Machines
  • Kitchen With Microwave, Fridge, Rice Cooker, AND Tea/Coffee
  • Machine
  • Table Tennis Ground
  • Basketball Playing Ground
  • Parking Space
  • Adequate Classroom Spaces To Avoid Overcrowd In The Classrooms
  • Take Fresh And Cheap Fruits And Vegetable By Yourself And Our School Is Surrounded By Agricultural Land And Farms Also Linyi City Has Competitive Vegetables And Fruits Industries In China. On Weekends Our Masters Take Students To Farms To Buy Cheap And Fresh Vegetables And Fruits
